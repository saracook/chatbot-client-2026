**Carina is free to use**. Services and hardware used to maintain Carina are currently fully subsidized for Stanford researchers. Principal Investigators (PIs) can start by [requesting a project](/request-carina-project).

The standard Carina setup includes 1TB of data storage for each project. 

Additional storage may be purchased in 10TB units at a rate of $97.50/month.

[Extra storage request form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc2XLhD3WSCj-v6-jp5AJqz_wUrGRqEq782XEpn62JbtZ5NeQ/viewform?usp=sf_link) To order, please sign in to Forms using your Stanford account.
